How the Geico Car Repair Process Works

If you are insured with GEICO and your vehicle is damaged in an accident, the repair process begins with filing a claim. You can file online, through the mobile app, or by phone.

  • Report accident details
  • Upload photos of vehicle damage
  • Schedule inspection
  • Receive repair estimate
  • Select repair facility
  • Track repair progress

After approval, the repair shop begins work and coordinates directly with the insurance adjuster for payment.

Choosing an Approved Repair Shop

GEICO partners with Auto Repair Xpress® facilities nationwide. These shops offer faster inspections and guaranteed repairs.

Benefits of Approved Shops

  • Lifetime repair guarantee
  • Direct billing to insurance
  • Faster processing
  • Certified technicians

You may also choose your own repair shop if preferred.

Rental Car Coverage During Repairs

If your auto policy includes rental reimbursement, you can receive coverage for a rental car while your vehicle is being repaired.

  • Daily coverage limits apply
  • Coverage duration depends on repair timeline
  • Available only if added to your policy

How Long Do Repairs Take?

Repair time depends on several factors:

  • Severity of damage
  • Parts availability
  • Insurance approval time
  • Shop workload

Minor repairs may take 3–5 days, while major collision damage can take several weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I choose my own repair shop?
Yes, you have the right to select any licensed repair shop.

Does GEICO guarantee repairs?
Repairs at approved facilities typically include a lifetime guarantee.

How do I check repair status?
You can track updates via your online insurance account.

Will insurance cover OEM parts?
Coverage depends on your policy details and state regulations.
